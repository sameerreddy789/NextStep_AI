What is NextStep AI?

NextStep AI is an AI-powered career guidance platform that helps users assess skills, prepare for interviews, and follow a personalized learning roadmap.

Who can use NextStep AI?

Students, freshers, career switchers, and professionals looking to upgrade their skills can use NextStep AI.

How does the interview assessment work?

The platform conducts adaptive interviews using MCQs and coding questions to evaluate technical, aptitude, and problem-solving skills.

Is NextStep AI free to use?

Core features are free to use, with advanced features planned for future releases.

How does the learning roadmap help?

The roadmap provides a structured, role-based learning plan with resources, practice tasks, and interview preparation steps.
